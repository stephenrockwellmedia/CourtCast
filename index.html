What We Build

THREE PRODUCTS.
ONE INTEGRATION.

Connect to CourtReserve once. Deploy across every screen in your facility — from the lobby wall to the end of each court.

Most Popular
📺

All Courts Overview

A full-facility live dashboard showing every court at a glance. Perfect for lobby TVs, front desk monitors, and entrance displays.

  • Live event name, time & status per court
  • Color-coded by activity or skill level
  • Upcoming event preview per court
  • Time remaining countdown per booking
  • Auto-refresh — zero staff input needed
  • Custom branding: colors + logo
🏆

Live Scoreboard

A full-screen scoreboard for a single court. Staff or players update scores from their phone — it reflects on-screen instantly via Firebase.

  • Large-format score display
  • Team names & player rosters from CourtReserve
  • Mobile controller for score input
  • Game and set tracking
  • Swap sides with one tap
  • Event info and round details
📡

Score Bug Overlay

A compact status bar at the bottom of any screen — overlaying a camera feed or any content. Sports-broadcast style, for your courts.

  • Court number, event, and player name
  • Time remaining & upcoming event
  • Live clock display
  • Transparent overlay on any background
  • Ideal for per-court wall displays
  • Works over live camera streams
Setup

LIVE IN AN AFTERNOON.

No custom hardware. No IT department. If you run CourtReserve, you're 90% of the way there.

01

Connect to CourtReserve

We tap into your existing CourtReserve API with read-only access. No data migration, no new software for your staff to learn.

02

We Configure Your Display

Your branded display pages go up with your club's colors, logo, and court layout. Each page is a simple URL opened on any screen.

03

Plug In Any Screen

Smart TV, Chromecast, Fire Stick, tablet, or any browser. No app installs, no special hardware required.

04

It Just Runs

Displays update automatically from your live CourtReserve schedule. Your staff never touches it — it's always current.

Score Bug

BROADCAST QUALITY.
AT COURT LEVEL.

Mount a screen at the end of each court. The Score Bug shows who's playing, time remaining, and who's up next — live from CourtReserve.

Player names and events come directly from your reservations. No manual entry at the court level.
